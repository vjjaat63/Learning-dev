The National Institute of Technology (NIT) Warangal is a prominent public technical university located in Warangal, Telangana, India. Established in 1960, it is one of the oldest NITs in India and is known for its strong emphasis on engineering and technology education. NIT Warangal offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering and science disciplines. The institute is also known for its research contributions, industry collaborations, and vibrant campus life.
