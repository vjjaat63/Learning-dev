Hyperlinks

Internal Hyperlinks

Internal hyperlinks connect different sections or pages within the same website. They enhance navigation and can improve SEO by linking related content. Examples include:

  • Navigational Links in menus or sidebars.
  • Contextual Links within content.
  • Related Content links at the end of posts.
  • Anchor Links to jump to specific sections.
  • Breadcrumbs for easy navigation.

External Hyperlinks

External hyperlinks direct users to a different website or web page outside the current site. They are used for:

  • Referencing and citing sources.
  • Providing additional resources.
  • Affiliate marketing.
  • Linking to third-party services.
Example:
